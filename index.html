SEEP Scholar Selection Process

Pure Merit-Based Selection

We believe in raw potential, not just past privilege. Our entire selection process is designed to find dedicated, hardworking students through a completely transparent, objective mechanism.

01

Qualifier Test

The gateway to SEEP. An objective assessment designed by academic experts to evaluate your fundamental aptitude, logical reasoning, and core conceptual clarity.

02

Merit Ranking

Based purely on the Qualifier Test performance, a transparent state-wide rank list is published. No hidden criteria, no interviews—just pure academic merit.

03

Seat Allocation

Top-ranked students are allocated their seats in the SEEP batches. Scholarships are directly linked to this rank, rewarding top performers instantly.
